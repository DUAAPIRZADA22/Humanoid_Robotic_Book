# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive course on **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**. This course will take you through the fascinating world of embodied intelligence, where artificial intelligence meets physical robotics to create systems that can interact with the real world.

## What You'll Learn

- **Foundations of Physical AI**: Understanding the principles that govern AI in physical systems
- **Humanoid Robot Design**: From mechanical structures to control systems
- **Machine Learning for Robotics**: Training AI models to control physical bodies
- **Computer Vision**: Enabling robots to perceive and understand their environment
- **Control Theory**: Mathematical foundations for robot movement and manipulation
- **Real-world Applications**: Explore how humanoid robots are transforming industries

## Course Structure

This course is divided into four comprehensive modules:

1. **Module 1: Foundations** - Introduction to Physical AI concepts and basic robotics
2. **Module 2: Perception** - Computer vision and sensor integration
3. **Module 3: Control** - Motion planning and control systems
4. **Module 4: Applications** - Real-world implementations and future directions

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming knowledge (Python preferred)
- Understanding of linear algebra and calculus
- Familiarity with machine learning concepts (helpful but not required)
- Passion for robotics and AI
